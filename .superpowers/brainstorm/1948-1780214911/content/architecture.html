Fluxo de polling

Apos disparar, o frontend consulta GET /repos/{owner}/{repo}/actions/runs?status=in_progress a cada 3s. Quando concluir, busca os logs via GET /repos/{owner}/{repo}/actions/runs/{id}/logs e exibe no terminal.
